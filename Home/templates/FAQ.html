How Much Does A Website Cost?

There are many factors that are considered when calculating the cost of a website such as:

  • How many pages you plan to implement to your website.
  • The features and services you would like to implement on your website.
  • The branding work that would need to be done on your website.
  • The amount of traffic you expect to generate on your website.

Some of the post-launch (per-monthly) expenses you can expect would include:

  • Per-month hosting operation cost via AWS.
  • SEO Research & Branding

What Does Our Process Entail?

1. Initial Consultation

Through our initial consultation, we take our time to get to know your and your business. We analyze your needs and present you a project scope and initial price.

2. Design Prototypes

During this phase of the project we present you with our design prototypes, you tell us what you like, what you don't like and what changes you want made to the design of your website.

3. Design & Development

This is the phase of the project where the rubber really meets the road. Where we put our head down and work until your solutions have been met to your satisfaction, revisions are required on your end.

4. Cloud Hosting

This is where your project is fully brought to life and only until all revisions have been made is your project hosted on our cloud hosting provider.

5. Continued Partnership

Just because your website is live does not mean that this is the end of the road, we continue our partnership with every single one of our clients.
