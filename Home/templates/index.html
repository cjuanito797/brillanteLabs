About Me

Remodeling Dreams Construction

The need for a website for Remodeling Dreams Construction came about when as soon as I realized that there WordPress built website was down and was affected by poor security measures. Per my initial conversation with Kari the co-owner of RDC, it became quite clear that they were highly unsatisfied with a website that gave them little to no control over managing their content. It was very static to say the least not to mention the security issues and hacking that they had faced in the past. They had no media control, a very disorganized method for keeping track of their quote leads and a lack of mobile responsiveness. The solution? A website that allows my client to be able to manage their services, applicants, and quote leads. They can create new galleries for their projects that they complete and the website itself is mobile and tablet friendly.
