A Little About Me.

Hello there, my name is Juan Lizarraga-Cortez, and I am a freelance web developer based out of the midwest. I established Code By Juan (formerly known as Prairie Code) in 2023. I discovered my passion for web development and programming during my undergraduate tenure at the University of Nebraska Omaha. I found myself being very entrepreneurial with the projects we were assigned, from launching a restaurant ordering business, to creating a social media site for local book users to creating a scheduling application for a local nail salon company. You can read more about me and my experience, at my "About Me" page below.
